Who am I?

I'm a 26 years old Brazilian lady, who is passionate about traveling, and I'm always trying to figure out the next place to go. I'm also a college student freaking out about how to pay for my degree, and planning helps me center myself - especially daydreaming about moving to another country and if that would be an option.

So I've decided to create a space where I can share my crazy research, and maybe help others find the information they need easier!

So, if you are like me, welcome to my craziness! And please join me, where we can dream and research our next adventure together! If there is another country or place you would like to learn more about it, make sure to send me a message with your request. Tchau!
