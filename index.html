Welcome to Florence, Italy!

Florence was founded as a Roman military colony about the 1st century bce, and during its long history it has been a republic, a seat of the duchy of Tuscany, and a capital (1865–70) of Italy. During the 14th–16th century Florence achieved preeminence in commerce and finance, learning, and especially the arts. Fun fact: The piano was invented in Florence!

More than 15 million visitors explore Florence each year, and many have migrated to either pursue a career or a more affordable education option than in the US.

University Costs

If you live in the United States, you know it can be overwhelming to figure out how to pay for college without going into debt for the rest of your life. However, I'm going to shown you that you can get a degree without needing to break your bank; and learn about another culture by living and going to college in a complete different country - and it would be cheaper than 1 year tuition in the US.

A Bachelor degree in Italy takes 3 years to complete, instead of 4. Their tuition is affordable, even if you are an international student.

For comparison, let's see the difference between a tuition in Fresno State vs the University of Florence:

Fresno State: $6,589.00 per semester vs. University of Florence: $1,730 per semester.

So, if you go to Fresno State for 4 years, you would spend a total of $26,356.00, while if you went to University of Florence as an international student, you would pay in 3 years $5,190.00 (less than the cost of one year!!).

Fun fact: the University of Florence offers classes in English.
